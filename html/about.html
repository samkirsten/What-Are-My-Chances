What Are My Chances?


About

What Are My Chances is a not for profit website that aims to make NHS open data accessable to the general public in a way that has never been done before.

It takes NHS open data from the Heath & Social Care Information Centre, Dr Foster Intelligence, Governmental Reports and NHS England Freedom of Information Requests; and uses this information to make an anaylsis of every hospital in England. This information is hard to come by and we are trying to gather statistics for every hospital in England, if you can help please get in touch at: Sam Kirsten @ Twitter

Hosting isn't free... any donations for our site would be greatly accepted. We are not for profit, we make no money out of the work that we do here. If you wish to sponsor us and have your company name listed in the footer, get in touch via the link above.
